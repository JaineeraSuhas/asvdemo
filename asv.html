Signal Pipeline

How ASV Works

10-stage pipeline from raw muscle signal to spoken word — happening in under 500 milliseconds.

01
EMG Signal Acquisition
Ag/AgCl surface electrodes on masseter (jaw sides) and suprahyoid (under chin) muscles detect microvolt-level electrical activity during silent speech. 3 electrode points. Sampling rate 1000–2000 Hz.
Ag/AgCl Electrodes 1000–2000 Hz sampling 0.1–10 mV range
02
Amplification
INA128 instrumentation amplifier boosts the tiny signal by 1000× while rejecting electromagnetic noise using differential electrode setup.
INA128 Gain = 1000× CMRR > 90 dB
03
Bandpass Filtering (20–500 Hz)
4th-order Butterworth filter removes movement artifacts below 20 Hz and high-frequency noise above 500 Hz. Implemented in both hardware and software.
Butterworth 4th Order 20–500 Hz passband SciPy
04
Notch Filter (50 Hz)
Eliminates Indian power-line interference at 50 Hz using a sharp IIR notch filter. Q factor = 30 for precise rejection without affecting EMG signal.
IIR Notch Q=30 50 Hz rejection
05
Rectification & Envelope Detection
Full-wave rectification followed by 20ms moving-average envelope creates a smooth activation profile showing when muscles are firing.
Full-wave Rectifier 20ms window NumPy
06
Windowing & Segmentation
200ms sliding windows with 50ms overlap. Hanning window applied per segment to reduce spectral leakage before frequency analysis.
200ms window 50ms overlap Hanning window
07
Feature Extraction (~40D vector)
Time-domain: MAV, RMS, ZCR, Waveform Length. Frequency: Mean Power, Median Frequency, STFT. Cepstral: 13 MFCCs. Combined ~40-dimensional feature vector per window.
MAV · RMS · ZCR · WL 13 MFCCs STFT
08
Normalisation & PCA
Z-score normalisation across sessions. Optional PCA reduces to top 20 components for lightweight inference on ESP32 microcontroller.
Z-score norm PCA → 20D Scikit-learn
09
ML Classification
Trained CNN-LSTM model classifies intended word from feature vector. Confidence threshold 0.75 — below that, returns "unclear" to avoid false positives.
CNN-LSTM Hybrid TensorFlow Lite Conf. threshold 0.75
10
Output — Text & Speech
Classified word sent via BLE to phone/PC. TTS engine speaks it aloud. Consecutive duplicate detection (300ms hold-off) prevents stuttered output. Sentence assembled word-by-word.
BLE GATT TTS Engine 300ms debounce Sentence assembly
Wearable Design

Electrode Placement

No mouth insertion. No surgery. Just 3 small gel sticker pads on your skin — under the chin and on the jaw sides.

SIDE VIEW MASSETER JAW MUSCLE THROAT-REF ✗ NOT inside mouth ✓ On skin surface only FRONT VIEW MASSETER-L MASSETER-R SUPRAHYOID THROAT-GND EMG SIGNAL RAW EMG (mV) WORD 1 WORD 2 FILTERED + ENVELOPE OUTPUT: "HELLO" conf: 0.94 · latency: 312ms
🦷
Masseter (Jaw Sides)
Two electrodes placed on the sides of the jaw, just in front of the ears. Feel it flex when you clench your teeth — that's the masseter. Strongest EMG source for jaw movements.
Position: Cheek-jaw junction · Bilateral · 32mm Ag/AgCl
🫦
Suprahyoid (Under Chin)
One electrode placed directly under the chin on the soft fleshy area. Activates during tongue movement, swallowing, and vowel formation. Key for word differentiation.
Position: Submental region · Midline · Captures tongue muscle signals
🎙️
Throat Reference (Ground)
One neutral ground electrode on the front of the throat, below the Adam's apple. Used as an electrical reference point to cancel common-mode noise from both active channels.
Position: Anterior neck · Reference/GND · Below thyroid cartilage
✅
Wearing Experience
Feels like three small sticky bandages on your skin. Takes under 3 minutes to apply. Wearable for 8+ hours. No pain, no irritation with hypoallergenic gel pads.
Hypoallergenic · Reusable strap · Setup <3 min
Full Feature Set

Everything ASV Does

From silent word detection to sentence assembly — here's every capability built into the system.

🧠
Silent Word Detection
Detects 50+ words from jaw/throat EMG signals during silent mouthing — no sound produced by the user required.
📝
Sentence Assembly
Words classified sequentially and assembled into full 7–8 word sentences in real time with NLP-based auto-correction.
🔊
Text-to-Speech
Classified words spoken aloud through phone or PC speakers using natural TTS engine within <200ms of classification.
📱
Phone & PC Output
Flutter Android app and Electron desktop app receive text over BLE. Real-time display on any connected device, wirelessly.
⚡
Real-Time Processing
End-to-end latency under 500ms from muscle contraction to spoken output. ESP32 runs inference locally — no cloud needed.
🎯
User Personalisation
15-minute calibration session adapts the model to individual muscle patterns. Each user gets their own fine-tuned profile.
🔋
All-Day Battery
2000mAh Li-Po provides 6–8 hours of continuous use. USB-C charging via TP4056. Full charge in 2.5 hours.
📡
Bluetooth Low Energy
BLE GATT protocol streams classified words to phone/PC with 15m range and minimal power draw from the wearable.
🏥
Non-Invasive & Safe
Surface gel electrodes only. No surgery, no implants, no pain. Designed for IEC 60601-1 medical electrical safety compliance.

Machine Learning

ML Model Strategy

Starting simple, scaling to state-of-the-art. Each phase uses a more powerful model as data and compute grows.

SVM Classifier
75%
Phase 1 · Baseline
Hand-crafted features + Support Vector Machine. Fast, low compute, works on ESP32 directly.
CNN-1D
88%
Phase 2 · Prototype
1D Convolutional network on raw/spectrogram windows. Strong pattern recognition across time.
LSTM / GRU
91%
Phase 2–3 · Beta
Recurrent network models the sequential nature of EMG time-series naturally.
CNN-LSTM Hybrid
93%
Phase 3 · Beta
CNN extracts features, LSTM models sequences. Best of both architectures combined.
Transformer-lite
95%+
Phase 4 · Launch
Attention mechanism over EMG time steps. Converted to TFLite for on-device inference.
LLM Post-processing
Context AI
Phase 4 · Enhancement
Lightweight LLM fixes word order, predicts next word from sentence context, corrects classification errors.
